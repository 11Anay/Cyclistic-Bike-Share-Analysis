# 🚴 Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes more than **5.6 million bike-share trips** to understand the behavioral differences between **casual riders** and **annual members**.

The objective is to identify data-driven strategies that can help Cyclistic convert casual riders into annual members.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Cyclistic wants to increase annual memberships by understanding how casual riders use the bike-sharing service differently from existing members.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Casual riders have a significantly longer average ride duration than annual members.
- Casual riders use bikes more frequently on weekends.
- Annual members show consistent weekday commuting behavior.
- Bike usage reaches its highest levels during the summer months.

---

## ✅ Business Recommendations

- Launch weekend membership campaigns targeting casual riders.
- Run seasonal promotions during summer.
- Introduce rewards and incentives for long recreational rides.
- Encourage casual riders to convert into annual members through personalized offers.
